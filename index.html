Welcome to my personal website!
I am a research associate in the School of Engineering at the University of Edinburgh. My current research interests are in areas of fluid dynamics, biological flows, microfluidics, reactive flows, suspension flows. I use computational methods, such as the lattice Boltzmann method to understand such complex fluid flow phenomena.
